Get ready for an electrifying season of Pro Kabaddi League! The league that has captivated millions across the globe is back in its 11th edition, promising even more intense action, jaw-dropping skills, and unforgettable moments. Witness the best kabaddi players from India and around the world battle it out for the ultimate title. With 12 teams vying for the championship, the competition is set to be fiercer than ever. Watch your favorite teams, including Bengaluru Bulls, Bengal Warriorz, Dabang Delhi K.C., Gujarat Giants, Haryana Steelers, Jaipur Pink Panthers, Patna Pirates, Puneri Paltan, Tamil Thalaivas, Telugu Titans, U Mumba & UP Yoddhas showcase their incredible skills. Pro Kabaddi League, a joint venture by Mashal Sports and Disney Star, has revolutionized the sport of kabaddi. For the past 10 years, the league has consistently delivered thrilling matches, nurtured young talent, and elevated kabaddi to new heights.
